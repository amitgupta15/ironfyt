FAQ

Short, honest answers. Keep it simple.

Do I need a barbell?

No. IronFyt loves barbells, but workouts can be scaled to dumbbells, machines, cables, or minimal equipment. The goal is capability, not perfection.

Is this for beginners?

Yes. The programming is scalable for every age and level. Modify movements, reduce load, and build up over time.

What does IronFyt track?

Workouts, sets, reps, load ranges, total volume moved, and trends over time—plus weekly and monthly consistency.

How does Premium work?

Premium unlocks full history, advanced movement analytics, PR tracking, and unlimited share cards. You can cancel anytime.
